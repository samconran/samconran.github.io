ABOUT

We are Black Peaks. We consist of:

Will Gardner — vocals
Joe Gosney — guitar
Andrew Gosden — Bass
Liam Kearley — drums

We like to blend progressive rock, metal, punk, and math-rock to create interesting music. Our debut album, Statues, was released in 2016, and we are currently working on our sophomore record ready for 2018.
